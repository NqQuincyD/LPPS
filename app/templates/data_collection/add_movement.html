Monthly HQ Graph Data

Collect this data from the monthly train graphs at HQ headquarters.

Required Information:
  • Train Number: Unique identifier from graph
  • Locomotive Number: Engine identification
  • Stations: Departure and arrival points
  • Times: Exact departure and arrival times
  • Load: Total weight in tons
  • Wagons: Number of wagons attached
  • Distance: Route distance in kilometers
Tips:
  • Double-check all numbers from the graph
  • Ensure times are accurate
  • Verify station names are correct
  • Check load calculations
